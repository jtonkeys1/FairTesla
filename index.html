FairTesla

A Community Driven Charity/Giveaway Project

FairTesla is a community controlled project with the aim of creating a deflationairy token with automatic contribution to a charity or giveaway that will be voted upon by the community.

Get FairTesla!

Giveaway or Charity!

Using a decentralized governance & consensus model, the community will be able to vote on giveaways & charity contributions, as well as updates on FairTesla. Our goal is to provide the best experience for all users & we believe letting the users vote on future updates is the best model. Once the 1% pool wallet is larged enough & unlocked, the community will vote on how to spend the money! A giveaway, charity contribution, & other future options!
