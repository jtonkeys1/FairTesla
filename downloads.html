Abstract

In the ordinary digital streaming music platform ecosystem you have multiple different layers all coordinating with each other. It starts with the artist. They have a song or EP/Album that they have created & want to release. In the current Web 2.0 infrastructure, there are a few ways to release this particular music asset to the public. You can use platforms like YouTube & other video hosting platforms, you can upload to Soundcloud or other audio hosting website, or you can use a “Distribution Aggregator” such as DistroKid, TuneCore, or other similar distribution platforms. What these current distribution platforms allow is simple. In exchange for a fee, they will have your music published to Spotify & Apple Music & a slew of other DSPs (digital streaming platforms). The way an artist is paid in the current model is determined by the streaming & distribution aggregate platform using a complex set of algorithms & variables. This always leave the artist with little to no pay, on average $.003-.001 per stream. This is extremely unfair to the artist & is only caused by greed & inefficiency of an outdated industry model. Our vision with Stera is to create a decentralized DSP that is self contained. One where artists fully control the rights & masters of their work and can also control value, scarcity, & availability of their music assets on a blockchain, all within a seamless UI just like any mainstream DSP such as Spotify or Apple Music. Some of the things we have in mind are: A decentralized DSP powered with a native Stera token on the Cardano blockchain (following Goguen release), use of the Stera token for payment for streaming (& other coming features) instead of your traditional subscription model, use of the Stera token to payout artist revenue with digital music assets uploaded & streamed by fans on the Stera platform, use of the IPFS & FIL/SIA blockchains to handle a peer to peer distributed decentralized backend database, direct artist portal to freely upload digital music assets, NFTs, merch, & other assets alongside a fully accessible Apple & Android native app similar to other streaming platforms. We also plan to introduce capabilities for potential live performances & exclusive video releases down the road. Combining all of these features & technology allow us to create a platform that is artist & community controlled, where the full royalties & artist revenue is collected & captured by the artist, and last but not least, a community of artists & music lovers that can fully own & control their entire music catalog. This allows full transparency between artist & consumer knowing that the money is going directly to the artist without any middle man or distribution aggregator gatekeeping the artist from receiving their rightfully earned revenue. By using 3rd generation blockchain tech combined with community governance & consensus stacks, the entire Stera ecosystem & it’s future will be controlled by the users & community. In the music industry, the labels & gatekeepers have made trampled small & independent artist. With recent developments in generation three blockchains & other achievements in the decentralized space, we believe it is possible to decentralize the music industry & give it back to artist who rightfully deserve their fair share. We hope to achieve all of this & more.
