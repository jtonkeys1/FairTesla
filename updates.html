Q1 2021

Q1 2021 includes the following:

Mainnet Launch
  • Launched FairTesla to the Binance Smart Chain Mainnet with 1,000,000,000,000,000 Total Supply.
Full Renouncement & LP locked
  • Renounced ownership of 1% community giveaway pool & DX Sale/PancakeSwap Liquidity Pools.

Q2 2021

Q2 2021 includes the following:

  • First community driven Tesla Giveaway!
  • Multiple community driven cash giveaways!
