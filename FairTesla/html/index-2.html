A platform built for artists & their communities

Since the beginning of the music industry, artists have continually been under-represented & under-paid relative to their total contributions. We built Stera with the sole purpose of providing a transparent, honest, & community driven platform so artists reap the full rewards of their work. By using Stera, you will be able to upload your music & have accurate metrics on total plays, total fans, total revenue, & many more that cannot be tampered, falsified, or edited. Artists are finally able to recieve full payouts of their work, real metrics that can be verified on the blockchain, & control their work completely. No more cuts taken by distribution aggregates, no more middle men. Directly from you, to your fans.

Fairness & accessibility for all

It is time artists & fans control their music & revenue without labels, distribution aggregators, managers, or any other third parties taking tiny percentages along the way. The community & artist will be in total control over the Stera ecosystem.

Extremely Secure

Using quantam proof blockchains, you can sleep knowing your music & metrics are untouchable & permanantly recorded in an untamperable way.

Artist/Fan Community

As an artist, you can engage with fans on a more direct way. Without a need from middle men, from you straight to your community.

Native iOS & Android App

With the Stera app on iOS or Android, you have the ability to stream music, show exclusive artist content, & have an exclusive community page just for fans!

No Third Parties

By using Stera, you will have complete control over your music & other assets you may release, with ZERO third-parties involved.

Transparency

Stera runs on a public blockchain ledger & the IPFS system making everything transparent & untamperable once published. No more fake data or falsified revenue models.

Community Controlled

Using built in governance & consensus models, the community & artists will control the future of Stera, including updates, future revenue models, etc. Nothing can be changed without community consensus voting.
